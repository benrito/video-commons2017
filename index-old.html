Why We're Getting Together

This workshop will build better relationships between stakeholders (platforms, producers, educators, funders, and users) working with open content and open video. We will jointly develop flagship projects to move faster as a field. We will tell our stories about what becomes possible when we deploy public infrastructure for open video.

Goal #1: Tools

Build and deploy tools that facilitate open licensing, open distribution, remix and editing of open video content.

Goal #2: Content

Produce projects, such as MOOCs or documentaries, open by default – in ways that are completely “open sourced” with metadata and licensing down to each individual asset. Document the benefits to producers and facilitate compliance with open access mandates.

Goal #3: Education

Pilot educational models for learner engagement with open video, including learning through video production and remix.
