VIDEO & THE COMMONS 2016

January 19th, 2016 9:30am - 4:00pm / 721 Broadway, New York - 4th Floor

This workshop will build better relationships between stakeholders (platforms, producers, educators, funders, and users) working with open content and open video. We will tell our stories about what becomes possible with public infrastructure, and develop flagship projects to move the field forward.

Morning: Tools

Collaborate to build and deploy tools that facilitate open licensing, open distribution, remix and editing of open video content.

Lunch: Content

Imagine projects, such as MOOCs or documentaries, open by default – with open metadata and licensing for each asset.

Afternoon: Education

Sketch out pilots to test models for video in learning, including student production & remix. Explore benefits for ecosystem.
