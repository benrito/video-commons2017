VIDEO & THE COMMONS 2016

January 19th, 2016 9:30am - 4:00pm / 721 Broadway, New York - 4th Floor

This workshop will build stronger relationships between stakeholders (platforms, producers, educators, funders, and users) working with open content and open video. We will tell our stories about what becomes possible with public infrastructure, and develop flagship projects to move the field forward.

Tools

Build and deploy tools that facilitate open licensing, open distribution, remix and editing of open video content.

Content

Produce projects, such as MOOCs or documentaries, open by default – with open metadata and licensing for each asset.

Education

Pilot educational models for learner engagement with open video, including learning through video production & remix.
